<h2>Why Work for Denver Health?</h2>
	<!-- Image wrap !-->
<div style="text-align: left;"><a href="https://denverhealth.wd1.myworkdayjobs.com/DHHA-Main/job/Rita-Bass-Trauma-Institute/XMLNAME--Denver-Health-Spring-Career-Fair_R206-2" target="_blank" id="May 16 2023 Hiring Event"><figure class="image-snippet left">
<div><img alt="test" src="-/media/images/blog-images/dhsm2010-65-frontline-workers-jeffreysankoff.jpg" style="height:350px; width:350px;" />                      </div>
</figure>
</a>
<p><span>Take your career to the next level at Denver Health, where we offer a robust benefits package and endless opportunities for growth. Denver Health is a nationally-ranked, locally-trusted, premier health care institution located in the heart of Denver, Colorado.</span></p>
</div>
	
<p style="text-align: left;">Nearly twenty-five percent of all Denver residents, or approximately 150,000 individuals, receive their health care here. We are known as an&nbsp;integrated health care system that encompasses multidisciplinary academic specialties, a community health system,&nbsp;a <a href="/services/trauma"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">level 1 trauma center</span></a>, <a href="/services/emergency-medicine/pediatric-emergency-urgent-care"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">pediatric emergency and urgent care center</span></a>, and is home to <a href="/services/community-health/public-health"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Denver Public Health</span></a> and many of the nation's leaders in medicine.</p>
	<div>
		
<p>Denver Health is an integrated, high-quality academic health care system considered a model for the nation that includes a Level I Trauma Center, a 555-bed acute care medical center, Denver&rsquo;s 911 emergency medical response system, 10 family health centers, 19 school-based health centers, Rocky Mountain Poison &amp; Drug Safety, Public Health Institute, an HMO and The Denver Health Foundation.</p>
	
<p>Located near downtown Denver, Denver Health is just minutes away from many of the cultural and recreational activities Denver has to offer.</p>
		
<p>&nbsp;</p>
	
	
<h3>Our Mission</h3>
	<!-- Image wrap !-->
<figure class="image-snippet right">
<div><img src="-/media/images/blog-images/dhsm2010-65-frontline-workers-dianahernandez.jpg?h=300&amp;w=300&amp;hash=B1DD5DFEA1515AA2ADA31BD26404ED84" style="height:350px; width:350px;" alt="Frontline Workers Diana Hernandez" />                      </div>
</figure>
	
<p>As Colorado&rsquo;s primary, and essential, safety-net institution, Denver Health is a <a href="/about-denver-health/community-impact"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">mission-driven organization</span></a>&nbsp;that has provided billions in uncompensated care for the uninsured. Denver Health is viewed as an Anchor Institution for the community, focusing on hiring and purchasing locally as applicable, serving as a pillar for community needs, and caring for more than 185,000 individuals and 67,000 children a year.</p>
