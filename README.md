<h2>Welcome to Denver Health</h2>
	
<p>Denver Health prioritizes your health and well-being through a comprehensive range of health care services designed to meet the diverse medical needs of the community. Our commitment to excellence is evident in our Level 1 Trauma Center, ensuring you receive prompt and expert attention in critical situations. Additionally, we offer convenient options such as walk-in and virtual urgent care , along with more than 150 specialty services.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Amenities &amp; Parking</h2>
<p>Denver Health offers patient and visitor amenities to make your visit as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. In 2021, Denver Health opened a brand new outpatient medical center equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and services including x-ray, ultrasounds available on the same day or next day, as well as CT and MRI scans. Denver Health provides full-service medical care with appointments, imaging, lab testing, and pharmacy services are accessible on the main campus. Patients also have access to Denver Health's multiple locations across the Denver area for further convenience.</p>
